Access Picture in Picture

With Picture in Picture, you can be in a different application while watching a YouTube video.

You can access Picture in Picture mode from the Mini Player, as well as Default View.
  1. Note: You may not see the Picture in Picture icon. This is because Picture in Picture icon fades after a few seconds. Try hovering over the video again.
    Hover over the video. The Picture in Picture icon should appear to the bottom right of the screen.
  2. Press the Picture in Picture icon.
