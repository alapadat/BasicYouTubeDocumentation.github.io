Choose Language

You can change YouTube's default subtitle language to another language.

  1. Press the Settings cog.
  2. Select the Subtitles/CC tab.
  3. Choose a language.
To customize subtitle font, color, opacity, etc, selectOpen Subtitle Options
  • Open Subtitle Options
    You can customize subtitle font, color, opacity, etc, depending on your personal preferences.
