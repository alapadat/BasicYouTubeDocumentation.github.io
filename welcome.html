YouTube is a free app where creators of all kinds can publish videos to their Channels. It gives you easy access to a vast range of content. You can Like or Comment on videos to express your opinion. If you want to see more videos from a creator, Subscribe to their channel.

  • Interact With a Video
    YouTube offers features that help you interact with a video better. You can Play/Pause a video, turn Subtitles and Autoplay on and off, and more. YouTube's features do a good job of giving you control without overwhelming you.
  • Find Videos
    Finding videos is easy with Youtube. But since Youtube is so large, finding exactly what you want can be tough.
  • Useful Information
    This information is not necessary to operate YouTube as a beginner. Being familiar with YouTube terminology and its algorithm helps you navigate the platform. Video creators and regular users may reference this information. This section is meant to give you insights into YouTube culture and a high-level understanding of the website's code.
  • Turn on Notifications
    If you really want to see a channel's content and updates from a channel, turn on notifications.
  • Subscribe to a Channel
    If a creator puts out content you like, subscribe to their channel to see more!
